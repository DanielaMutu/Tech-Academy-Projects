Visit amaizing places in Romania

It is the period for traveling and I recommend you to travel Transfăgărasan route in Romania.

The wonderful curves of Transfăgărășan were built during 4 years between 1970-1974.
This route takes place on the Făgărașului Mountains and unites Muntenia with Transylvania.

It is a wild road, where you breathe a sweet and cool mountain air, where the steep mountainous
form blends harmoniously with the road,and landscapes feed your soul and remain in your memory.
